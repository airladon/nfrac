Instructions

Change the maximum number of iterations with the slider.

Change the number of polynomial roots with the Roots button.

Reset zoom and pan with the Home button.

Animate to different root presets with the Presets button.

View a heat map of the complex numbers that took the longest to converge with the Iterations to Converge toggle.
